In the contemporary landscape, data emerges as the quintessential instrument for
fostering sustainability, embarking us on an enlightening odyssey to achieve
sustainable excellence. This expedition leverages the profound potential of data
to propel environmental, societal, and economic sustainability. Through data-driven
insights, organizations and individuals can judiciously navigate, optimizing the
allocation of resources and pinpointing areas ripe for enhancement, thereby curtailing waste, conserving precious energy, and mitigating ecological repercussions.
From vigilant monitoring of greenhouse gas emissions to the meticulous refinement of supply chains and the advocacy of eco-friendly practices, data serves as the guiding compass, leading us toward an eminently sustainable future. Data scientists and
statisticians are the sentinels of a data-driven society that is influenced not just
by ones and zeros but also by the enduring ideals of sustainability and conscience.
In this symbiotic relationship, data not only empowers innovation but also
guarantees that our commitment to sustainability is not just a mere objective but a relentless and data-fueled quest for unassailable excellence.

General Guidelines

  • Any student pursuing a postgraduate degree in Statistics, Data Science, Mathematics, Computer Science can participate.
  • All participants are requested to get a permission letter from their respective institutions and ID cards to participate in the event.
  • Participants are requested to register beforehand.
  • Participating teams/participants may lodge objections on technical matters within 30 minutes of the completion of the event.
  • Participants are expected to be in a formal dress code.
  • Participants are requested to be present at the venue of their events 15 minutes before the event starts. If eliminated, participants are welcome to participate in other events, keeping the time constraints in mind.
  • Participants can participate in more than one event simultaneously, given that they manage their event schedule accordingly.
